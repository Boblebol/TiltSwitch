Tiny app. Big privacy posture.

TiltSwitch uses the camera only inside the local app process. Frames are not recorded, stored, uploaded, analyzed remotely, or turned into a dashboard nobody asked for.

Local Vision

Apple Vision reads face roll angle on your Mac. No server, no account, no background mystery.

Menu bar only

No Dock icon, no big window, no “productivity hub”. Just a small status item.

Stops politely

The camera session is released when disabled, locked, sleeping, or quitting.

No dependencies

Pure Swift, AppKit, SwiftUI, AVFoundation, Vision, and CoreGraphics.

How the magic trick works.

There is no magic. Just camera frames, a face roll angle, a cooldown, and the same Mission Control keyboard shortcuts you already know.
